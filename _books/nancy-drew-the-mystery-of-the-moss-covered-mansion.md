  In _The Mystery of the Moss-Covered Mansion_ (Nancy Drew Mystery Stories #18), young detective Nancy Drew finds herself embroiled in a high-stakes case along Florida’s east coast. While not a Great Loop book, this one has a fun Looper connection: it takes place on Florida’s east coast near Cape Kennedy. Reading it while cruising through the Space Coast or nearby waterways adds an extra layer of fun — imagining Nancy sneaking around the moss-covered mansion while you’re motoring past nearby islands.  

Kids will enjoy the mix of mystery and wild animals, while adults can appreciate the nostalgia and regional references, especially if you’ve explored this part of Florida by boat.
