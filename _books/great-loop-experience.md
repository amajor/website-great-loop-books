This is one of those books that helps you see the *full picture* of the Loop early on. Even though some of the details have changed over time, it’s still a solid way to understand what you don’t know yet... especially in the dreaming and early planning phase.

I’d pair it with more current resources (like active <a href="https://www.looplifeacademy.com/community" target="_blank">Looper communities</a> or recent cruising guides), but it does a great job of laying the groundwork and helping you start asking the right questions.
